About The Event

異質整合下的設計與測試挑戰

Design/Test Challenges in the Era of Heterogeneous Integration

在奈米時代的背景下，異質整合對IC設計帶來了前所未有的挑戰與機遇。
本論壇將深入探討在異質整合環境下，如何克服設計與測試中的複雜性，實現高效能與高品質的IC產品。
隨著技術不斷微縮，設計與測試的難度也隨之增加，這對產學界提出了更高的要求。
透過產學專家的經驗分享與前沿研究的展示，參與者將能夠了解最新的技術趨勢，提升專業能力，並促進產學合作，共同迎接未來的挑戰。

In the nano era, heterogeneous integration brings unique challenges and opportunities for IC design.
This forum will address how to tackle the complexities in design and testing to achieve high-performance, high-quality IC products.
As technology scales down, the demands on industry and academia increase.
Participants will gain insights into the latest trends, enhance their skills, and foster collaboration through expert insights and cutting-edge research presentations.
